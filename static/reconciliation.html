📋 CSV Format Guide
Your CSV must have a Date column and either a single Amount column (positive = deposit, negative = withdrawal) OR separate Debit and Credit columns. A Description column greatly improves auto-matching accuracy.
Format A (Single amount)
Date,Description,Amount,Balance
2026-05-01,CUSTOMER PAYMENT,1000.00,5000.00
2026-05-02,RENT PAYMENT,-1200.00,3800.00
Format B (Debit/Credit)
Date,Description,Debit,Credit,Balance
2026-05-01,CUSTOMER PAYMENT,,1000.00,5000.00
2026-05-02,RENT PAYMENT,1200.00,,3800.00
